Types of Cybersecurity Threats



  • Phishing Attacks: Social Engineering designed to steal user credentials
  • Malware: Programs intended to harm a computer system
  • Web Attacks: Using vulnerabilities in websites to steal data or cause damage
  • Other: Man-in-the-middle, insider threats, eavesdropping, and many more ways for criminals to attack your systems

Information for this page was sourced from Phoenix NAP, LLC. Please refer to their website for more detailed information.
